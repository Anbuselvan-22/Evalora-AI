Photosynthesis Evaluation Rubric

Total Marks: 20

Question 1: Explain the process of photosynthesis (20 marks)

Criteria:
1. Definition and Overview (4 marks)
   - Correct definition of photosynthesis
   - Mentions conversion of light to chemical energy
   - Identifies location (chloroplasts)

2. Process Steps (6 marks)
   - Lists at least 3 key steps correctly
   - Explains light absorption
   - Describes water splitting
   - Mentions CO2 fixation

3. Chemical Equation (4 marks)
   - Correctly writes the photosynthesis equation
   - Balances the equation properly
   - Identifies reactants and products

4. Importance and Factors (6 marks)
   - Explains importance for life
   - Mentions oxygen production
   - Lists at least 2 affecting factors
   - Describes two stages of photosynthesis

Scoring Guide:
- Excellent (16-20): Complete understanding with detailed explanations
- Good (12-15): Good understanding with minor gaps
- Average (8-11): Basic understanding with some errors
- Poor (0-7): Incomplete or incorrect understanding
